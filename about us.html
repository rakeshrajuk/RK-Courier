About RK Courier


The new normal of contactless deliveries are happening all around the Express Industry and we at RK are in sync with on time innovative solutions. We have logistic solutions for Business and Individuals in and around the country, and globally as well!!

Want to send a shipment from Chennai to Bangalore or Delhi to Mumbai or Kolkata to Jaipur or even Trichy to Canada or Mumbai to Singapore?

RK covers everything without you stepping out to send the shipment. Be it International or Domestic, the RK network serves you with the most convenient way to send your shipments anywhere in the world.

Ship safely with RK App and enjoy speed and convenience at your fingertips. We are also there to make a Big difference to Small businesses. You can reach out to your clients directly from your store through RK App.

We began as a courier service in 1990, and have now evolved into an end-to-end express logistics player with solutions.
